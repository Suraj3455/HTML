Welcome to Silver Oak Institute of Engineering (SOIE), a premier institution committed to delivering quality education in the field of engineering and technology. Since its establishment in 2010, SOIE has been dedicated to empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values that help them excel in academics, research, and industry.

At SOIE, we provide a blend of theoretical learning and practical experience through modern laboratories, advanced computer centers, digital libraries, and industry-oriented teaching methods. Our faculty members are highly qualified, experienced, and passionate about guiding students toward achieving their full potential.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in engineering education, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development of students to meet global challenges.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education in engineering and technology through a student-centric approach.
  • To promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students and faculty.
  • To collaborate with industry and academia for mutual growth and development.
  • To nurture ethical values, leadership skills, and social responsibility in students.

Why Choose Us?

  • Accredited programs and affiliation with Gujarat Technological University (GTU).
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities.
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty members.
  • Strong industry connections and placement support.
  • Focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Vibrant campus life with extracurricular activities and events.

Join us at Silver Oak Institute of Engineering and embark on a journey of learning, growth, and success. Together, we can shape a brighter future through education and innovation.
